# Recruiting Strategy

Top-level strategy for recruiting middle school earth-science teachers to Mission HydroSci. This document is the anchor — every other file in the repo is in service of one of the waves or channels described here.

---

## Cohort Targets

| Cohort | Size | Recruiting window | Locked by |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Fall 2026** | 50+ teachers | May–Jul 2026 | 2026-08-15 |
| **Spring 2027** | 50+ teachers (target) | Aug–Nov 2026 | 2026-12-15 |

A 50+ cohort in a 3-month window means parallel channels — single-channel campaigns will not hit the target.

---

## Channel Priority Ranking

Ordered by expected ROI (highest first). See linked files for execution detail.

1. **Pilot teacher referrals** — see [pilot-teacher-engagement.md](pilot-teacher-engagement.md). Highest ROI, lowest cost. Existing teachers refer 3–5 peers each.
2. **Earth-science organizations** — see [earth-science-organizations.md](earth-science-organizations.md). NESTA, NAGT, AGU, AGI, NSTA earth-section. Audience is exactly our target.
3. **Teacher-authored sites** — see [teacher-resource-sites.md](teacher-resource-sites.md). Sunrise Science (90k+ teacher reach), Science Spot, CRScience.
4. **State science teacher associations** — see [state-channels.md](state-channels.md). State NSTA chapters; many have research-recruitment newsletters.
5. **Online teacher communities** — see [online-teacher-communities.md](online-teacher-communities.md). Facebook groups, Reddit, Twitter #ngsschat.
6. **University and partnership channels** — see [university-and-partnership-channels.md](university-and-partnership-channels.md). Mizzou alumni network, BSCS, NextGenScience.
7. **Conferences** — see [conferences-and-events.md](conferences-and-events.md). NSTA regionals (fall), NESTA workshops, ISTE.
8. **District contacts** — see [district-contacts.md](district-contacts.md). Useful for institutional approval but slower than direct teacher outreach.
9. **Apple Education / Google for Education** — see [apple-education-contacts.md](apple-education-contacts.md), [chromebook-channels.md](chromebook-channels.md). Cross-district pattern info; warm intro pathway.

## Outreach Campaign Timeline (Fall 2026)

Anchored to today (2026-04-29).

### Wave 1 — Warm channels (May 2026)
- Pilot teacher activation (referrals, testimonials, peer intros)
- University of Missouri College of Education alumni network
- Direct cold outreach to teachers identified via `teacher-resource-sites.md`
- NESTA and NAGT initial outreach
- **Goal:** 25 qualified leads, 10 applications

### Wave 2 — Broad teacher channels (May–June 2026)
- Newsletter mentions on Sunrise Science Classroom and Science Spot
- Posts in Facebook groups, Reddit, Twitter #ngsschat
- State science teacher association outreach
- Webinar #1 (info session) — recorded for landing page
- **Goal:** 75 leads cumulative, 30 applications

### Wave 3 — Partnerships and co-marketing (June–July 2026)
- Apple Education Solutions Engineer intro request
- Google for Education outreach (Chromebook channel)
- BSCS / NextGenScience / Achieve partnership outreach
- Webinar #2
- **Goal:** 125 leads cumulative, 60 applications

### Wave 4 — Final push, screening, cohort lock (July–mid-Aug 2026)
- Final outreach on outstanding leads
- Screening calls and final selection
- Cohort kickoff communication
- Onboarding and training scheduling
- **Goal:** 50+ teachers in cohort, locked by 2026-08-15

### Wave 5 — Contingency (only if behind target)
Triggered if leads <75 by 2026-06-30 OR applications <30 by 2026-06-30:
- Paid promotion in teacher newsletters (We Are Teachers, Edutopia)
- Targeted Facebook / Instagram ads to earth-science teacher segments
- Direct paid sponsorship of an NESTA or NAGT communication
- Bounty referral program for pilot teachers

## Working Principles

- **Earth-science teachers are the unit, not districts.** District-level outreach is slower and lower-yield than reaching teachers directly through teacher-facing channels.
- **Stipend + PD credit + co-authorship is the strongest pitch.** See [value-proposition.md](value-proposition.md). The non-monetary incentives often matter more than the stipend at the margin.
- **iPads OR Chromebooks — both qualify.** Be inclusive in every outreach message. Chromebook-only schools are likely larger in number than iPad-only schools at the U.S. middle-school level.
- **Pilot teachers are the cheapest channel we have.** Every other channel costs us time or money; referrals from existing pilots cost almost nothing and convert at a much higher rate.
- **Track every contact.** A teacher who declines for Fall 2026 is a viable Spring 2027 candidate. Without tracking, we lose them.
