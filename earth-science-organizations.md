# Earth-Science Organizations

National organizations whose membership overlaps directly with our target audience: middle-school earth-science teachers in the U.S. These are the highest-priority channels in the campaign because the audience targeting is essentially perfect.

## Quick-Win Strategy (First 2 Weeks)

If pursuing only the highest-leverage moves:

1. **NESTA newsletter mention** — single most concentrated audience; pursue this first.
2. **NSTA "Science Scope" article pitch** — middle-school journal, directly relevant content angle.
3. **AGI Earth Science Week 2026 listing** — annual event reaching a broad earth-science teacher audience.

These three together would put MHS in front of essentially every earth-science middle-school teacher actively engaged with their professional community.
